DeepSoRo: Soft Robot Acoustic Sensing

May 2022 - July 2024

Junyang Tang

As a Research Assistant on the DeepSoRo: Soft Robot Acoustic Sensing project at New York University's AI4CE Lab (Advisor: Prof. Chen Feng), I developed a soft robotic system capable of multimodal perception and object description by integrating acoustic sensing and robotic vision. My work included designing an embedded-controlled 16-bit analog multiplexer, and programming the Raspberry Pi as the master controller to interface with sensors, DAC/ADC systems, and motors. I also recorded, analyzed, and filtered audio waveform signals using digital signal processing (DSP) techniques. In collaboration with the team, I contributed to data collection, dataset construction, and model training for object classification and tactile understanding.
