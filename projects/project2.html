10-bit SAR ADC Design, Tapeout, and Testing with Texas Instrument PDK

January 2025 - Now

Junyang Tang

Led a team to design, verify, tape out, and test a full transistor-level 10-bit Successive Approximation Register Analog-to-Digital Converter (SAR ADC) with Sample-and-Hold Amplifier (SHA) using the Texas Instruments CMOS PDK. Designed, simulated, and laid out all transistor-level blocks of the SAR ADC with SHA; achieved ~10.4 ENOB (pre-layout) and 10.17 ENOB (post-layout) for the SHA, ~9.6 → 9.5 ENOB for the SAR ADC, and ~9.3 ENOB for the integrated system, demonstrating minimal degradation after layout with full-chip PEX and DRC/LVS signoff. The physical silicon chip will be fabricated by Texas Instruments and tested by my team and me in Spring 2025. (Pictures cannot be posted due to Texas Instruments Intellectual Property Regulations).
