Hotel Service Robot

September 2020 - December 2020

Junyang Tang

As part of a project at New York University under the guidance of Prof. Rui Li, I developed a smart IoT hotel service robot that integrated AI-based robotic vision and NFC technology to autonomously assist guests with in-room services. The robot was designed with a focus on seamless human-robot interaction in hospitality environments, showcasing capabilities in smart navigation, object recognition, and secure contactless interactions. This project earned the Nick Russo Award (Top 2) in the NYU Rapid Assembly and Design Project Competition, recognizing its innovation and practical implementation.
