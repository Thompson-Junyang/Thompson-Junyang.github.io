Embodied AI

January 2023 - July 2024

Junyang Tang

As a Research Assistant and Team Lead for the Embodied AI: NYU Self Drive project at New York University's AI4CE Lab (Advisor: Prof. Chen Feng), I led the development and deployment of real-world navigation solutions, beginning with a Robot Perception teaching system and culminating in the creation of a final exam platform used by Robotics master's students and vertically integrated project teams. I spearheaded the implementation of high-performance visual navigation algorithms in resource-constrained simulation environments, and designed a Raspberry Pi controlled Embodied AI agent robot equipped with embedded visual sensors. The project also involved establishing Robot Operating System (ROS) communication on a master PC to coordinate autonomous navigation tasks and execute Visual Place Recognition (VPR) within a 3D-printed, real-world maze environment.
